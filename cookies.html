What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help websites remember information about your visit and may support basic functionality, performance and security.

How ClearCopy Clinic uses cookies

ClearCopy Clinic may use cookies and similar technologies for limited purposes such as:

  • basic performance and security
  • understanding general website traffic and usage patterns

ClearCopy Clinic does not use cookies to take control of your device or access files on your computer.

Third-party services

Some cookies may be set by third-party services used on the site, such as hosting providers, analytics tools or embedded services. These third parties may process data according to their own privacy policies.

Managing cookies

Most web browsers allow you to control, block or delete cookies through browser settings. Please note that disabling certain cookies may affect how some parts of the site function.
