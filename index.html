Pricing

ClearCopy Clinic uses per-word pricing for most projects, which makes quoting more predictable than an hourly model.

Most work falls in the region of R0.20 to R0.25 per word, depending on the document, deadline and level of editing required.

Urgent work is available at a premium, subject to capacity.

How it works

The process is designed to be straightforward, transparent and collaborative where needed.

Send the basic details

Email the document type, approximate word count, deadline and the kind of editing you need. You do not need to send the full document at this stage.

Receive a quote and timeline

You will receive a clear price and an estimated turnaround time, so you can decide whether you want to proceed.

Share the document if you accept

Once you are ready to go ahead, the document can be shared for editing. For larger or more complex jobs, communication remains open throughout the process.

Review the edits

Editing is handled transparently so that changes remain visible and reviewable. Your document remains your work at all times.
