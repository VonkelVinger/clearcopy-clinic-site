Academic Integrity & AI Use

ClearCopy.Clinic provides editing support only and does not engage in ghostwriting or content generation for submission.

Editing approach

All edits are intended to improve clarity, correctness, and presentation while preserving the author's original meaning.

AI tools

Digital tools may be used to assist with efficiency, but all substantive editorial decisions are reviewed by a human editor.

Turnitin and similar systems

No guarantees are made regarding similarity scores or AI detection results.

Client responsibility

The client remains responsible for ensuring that submitted work complies with institutional policies.
